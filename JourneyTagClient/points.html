Carry points are awarded each time you move a tag.

Photo points are awarded when another player up-votes your photo.




Carry Point Details

While carrying a tag, you earn x points per mile, depending upon payout. As your level goes up, your payout increases. See the payout section on the home screen.

Photo Point Details

You can award other players for taking good photos. Each photo up-vote will give 100 points to the other player.

Photo up-votes can be retracted at any time so this score may fluctuate.
