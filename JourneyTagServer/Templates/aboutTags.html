About Tags

The game of JourneyTag is all about traveling to a place you've never been. You do this by creating a "tag" and choosing a destination.

The tag is like a baton in a relay race. It gets passed from player to player until it reaches its destination. With each step, you get a photo and a mark on the map.

Always drop your tag in well populated areas, but they can be picked up within five miles of your current location, so don't be too picky.
